How To Play

Welcome to Dictionary Dash! Your objective is to provide a match to the given definition by inputting its corresponding word. You will have 60 seconds to rack up as many points as possible. When the timer reaches 0, the game ends. Don't worry though, with every correct answer, you'll earn an additional 5 seconds on your timer.
